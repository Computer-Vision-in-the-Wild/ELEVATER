What is ELEVATER?

The ELEVATER benchmark is a collection of resources for training, evaluating, and analyzing vision language models on image classification and object detection. ELEVATER consists of:

  • A benchmark suite that consists of 20 image classification datasets and 35 object detection datasets, augmented with external knowledge
  • An automatic hyper-parameter tuning toolkit that is developed to facilitate model evaluation on downstream tasks.
  • A public leaderboard for tracking performance on the benchmark

The ultimate goal of ELEVATER is to drive research in the development of Vision Language models.
